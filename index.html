Mile Easily

To run a mile quickly and easily, we need to focus on gradually increasing our distance and intensity to build our endurance. Start with short runs, include interval training to improve speed, and incorporate strength training to enhance power. With consistent training and the right technique, you'll be able to run a mile quickly and easily.
